Convention/Grammar

Prefixes: Prefixes are word parts added to the beginning of a base word to change its meaning, such as "dis-" in "disbelief" or "im-" in "impossible."

Words that I learnt from the story

Crew: A group of people who work together, especially on a ship or aircraft.
Sentence: The ship's crew worked hard to sail through the storm.

Uttered: Said something aloud.
Sentence: He uttered a quiet thanks to his friend.

Screech: A loud, high-pitched, and usually unpleasant sound.
Sentence: The car brakes made a loud screech when it stopped suddenly.
